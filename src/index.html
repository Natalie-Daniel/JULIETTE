Better living, Better life, Better YOU

Welcome to Bellamy Florence! We are OPEN and located
near the campus of the University of North Alabama - Roar Lions! Constructed in 2018, we're the newest off-campus housing for students in Florence.

Living here you will be a part of the most technologically advanced apartment community, fully loaded with smart
home features. See us at our brand new clubhouse and reserve your room today!

Every apartment is leased by the bed which means that
you rent a bedroom and share the common space with
other roommates. You can either come with roommates planned or we can help you match with new friends.
 

Availability Spring 2021 Semester

Life changes. Remain flexible. Need housing for the Spring Semester? We're your place. Socially distance in our spacious three, four and five bedroom apartments that are rented-by-the-bed.
